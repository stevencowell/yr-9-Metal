Week 13: Project Finalisation & Quality Assurance

Introduction

This week is all about giving your Carry-all the final polish and ensuring it meets your design, functional, and quality standards. You’ll complete a rigorous quality assurance check and prepare your project for presentation.

Final Assembly Checks

  • Re-tighten any loose screws or fasteners
  • Verify glue bonds are fully cured and secure
  • Ensure moving parts (lids, handles) operate smoothly

Quality Assurance Checklist

  • Squareness: measure diagonals; tolerance within 1–2 mm
  • Joint fit: no visible gaps
  • Surface finish: uniform sanding and finish application
  • Hardware: correct alignment, no stripped screws
  • Functionality: stable base, easy carry, tools secure

Documentation & Reflection

  • Complete your project sheet with measurements and materials used
  • Write a brief reflection on challenges faced and lessons learned
  • Take photos or annotate steps for your portfolio

Discussion Starters

  • Which QA check is most critical for your Carry-all?
  • How do you decide acceptable tolerances?
  • What did you learn that you will use in your next project?
