Week 15: Course Reflection & Next Steps

Introduction

Congratulations on completing the Carry-all unit! This final week is about reflecting on your journey, celebrating your achievements, and planning how to carry these skills forward.

Reflecting on Your Learning

  • Which three skills have you improved the most?
  • What was your biggest challenge, and how did you overcome it?
  • Which part of the process—marking, joinery, finishing—did you enjoy most?

Documenting Your Portfolio

  • Gather photos—show every stage from blank timber to final product.
  • Include quiz results and discussion notes as evidence of understanding.
  • Write a 100-word summary of your project story.

Next Steps

  • Identify one advanced joint or technique to learn next_term.
  • Plan a small personal project and apply these skills independently.
  • Join a local makerspace or woodworking club to continue practicing.
