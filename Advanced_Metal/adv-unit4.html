Unit 4: Marking‑out & Measuring Tools – Advanced Scenario

Case Study / Scenario

During a skills competition, teams were asked to produce a metal bracket with precisely spaced holes. One team relied solely on a steel rule and marker. The result was misaligned holes and an unusable part. Another team used marking‑out dye, a scriber, odd‑leg calipers and a centre punch, followed by drilling. Their bracket fit perfectly.

Later in the course, a student misread the vernier calliper when measuring a shaft’s diameter, resulting in an undersized component that would not fit its mating part. The mistake was not discovered until assembly, causing delays and material waste.
