Unit 9: CNC & Advanced Manufacturing – Advanced Scenario

Case Study / Scenario

Students designed a custom keyring using CAD software and generated toolpaths for a CNC mill. In the first run, they forgot to set the machine’s origin correctly. The tool plunged into the vice and broke. The error prompted a discussion about coordinate systems and machine setup.

Another group used a 3D printer to produce a prototype metal component. They printed it vertically to save space, but the layers separated when the part was tested. The instructor explained how print orientation and support structures affect strength and quality in additive manufacturing.
