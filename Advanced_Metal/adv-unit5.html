Unit 5: Cutting & Shaping Techniques – Advanced Scenario

Case Study / Scenario

Students were tasked with producing curved aluminium brackets. One pair used the guillotine and bending brake correctly and produced clean, consistent bends. Another pair attempted to cut the brackets with a hacksaw and file. Their cuts wandered off line, they wasted material and their brackets were not symmetrical. Later, they tried to use a nibbling machine without adjusting the clearance and jammed the cutter.

In a separate exercise, a student used a power shear to cut sheet metal but failed to keep their hands clear of the blade, narrowly avoiding injury. The incident highlighted the importance of machine set‑up and hand positioning.
