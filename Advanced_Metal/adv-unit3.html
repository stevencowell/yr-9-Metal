Unit 3: Materials – Ferrous & Non‑Ferrous Metals – Advanced Scenario

Case Study / Scenario

A community garden asked students to build durable outdoor benches. The team chose mild steel for strength but did not apply a protective finish. After a few months outdoors, the benches began to rust and stain the paving. They realised they had not considered corrosion resistance or alternative materials in their design.

Meanwhile, another group fabricated a lightweight toolbox from aluminium. It was easy to carry but dented easily during use. In the workshop, a student quenched a freshly forged steel chisel in water to save time. The chisel later chipped and cracked when used to cut steel.
