Advanced Theory: Adhesives Scenario

Dry‑fit checks use diagonal measurements: if diagonals match within 1 mm, the box is square. Racking forces are analysed as moments; a 2 mm diagonal error in a 300 mm frame produces ~0.38° skew. Trammel points or story sticks transfer measurements without cumulative tape errors. Moisture gradients across panel thickness can induce cupping; acclimating panels to workshop EMC minimises warping. High‑gap joints may be filled using epoxy plus micro‑balloons, but this is aesthetic—not structural.
