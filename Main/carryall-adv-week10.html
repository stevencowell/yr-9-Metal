Advanced Theory: Cutting Scenario

Common post‑assembly corrections include scraping glue squeeze‑out with a card scraper sharpened to a 0.8 mm burr. Slight twist (<1 mm per 300 mm) can be planed out using winding sticks. Butterfly (Dutchman) keys stabilise splits by adding cross‑grain shear resistance. Heat guns at 70 °C soften cured PVA for joint disassembly. Coloured toner in lacquer evens sapwood‑heartwood contrast; target Delta‑E colour variance <3 for furniture‑grade panels.
