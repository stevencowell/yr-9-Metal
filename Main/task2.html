Advanced Task 2: Materials Selection Case Study

You are designing a lightweight fishing rod holder that will be used near salt water. Two common metals considered are aluminium and mild steel. Answer the questions below to justify your choice of material.

Respond to the questions

  1. Compare the properties of aluminium and mild steel that are relevant to this application (e.g. weight, corrosion resistance, strength).

  2. Which metal would you choose for the rod holder? Justify your selection based on the properties discussed.

  3. Suggest a surface treatment or coating to further improve the durability of your chosen material and explain why.
