Advanced Theory: Design Scenario

Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander Peoples developed sophisticated timber technologies over tens of thousands of years, guided by deep ecological knowledge (often called Traditional Ecological Knowledge, TEK). Harvesting followed cultural protocols—taking bark only in the right season and from healthy trees so they regrow. Spinifex resin was used as a thermoplastic adhesive, reheated to repair tools. Respectful use of Indigenous designs today requires genuine collaboration and acknowledgement of intellectual and cultural property rights.
