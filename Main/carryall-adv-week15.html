Advanced Theory: Presentation Scenario

A professional portfolio documents project scope, technical drawings, LCA results, SPC data and high‑resolution photos. Captions follow the STAR method—Situation, Task, Action, Result. Continuous Professional Development (CPD) for trades may require 20 hours annually, tracked via logbook. Reflective practice uses Gibbs’ cycle: Description, Feelings, Evaluation, Analysis, Conclusion, Action Plan. Publishing case studies on platforms like Behance or LinkedIn builds professional presence.
