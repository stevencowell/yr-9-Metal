Advanced Task 1: Safety Scenario

Read the following scenario and answer the questions below. This exercise challenges you to apply risk management principles in a realistic workshop situation.

Scenario: During a metalworking class, a student is using an angle grinder to cut a piece of steel. They are wearing safety glasses but no face shield. Their long sleeves are loose, and there is a pile of oily rags nearby. Sparks from the grinder are landing on the rags.

Respond to the questions

  1. Identify at least three hazards present in the scenario.

  2. What control measures would you implement to reduce the risk of injury or fire?

  3. Explain why wearing appropriate PPE is essential when using power tools like an angle grinder.
