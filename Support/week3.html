Task 3: Material Properties Sort

Support Theory

Metals are broadly classified as ferrous (containing iron) or non‑ferrous (containing little or no iron). Ferrous metals like mild steel and cast iron are usually magnetic, strong and heavier but susceptible to rust. Non‑ferrous metals such as aluminium, copper and lead are generally lighter, malleable and resistant to corrosion. Being able to recognise and sort these materials is important when selecting stock for a project.
