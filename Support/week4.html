Task 4: Measure & Mark

Support Theory

Accurate measuring and marking are vital in metalwork. Use the right tool for the job: a steel rule for measurements, a scriber for fine scratches, a try square for right angles and odd‑leg calipers for drawing parallel lines from an edge. Marking‑out blue can be applied to surfaces so your scribed lines stand out clearly. A centre punch makes a small dimple to guide the drill and prevent wandering.
