Week 4: Materials & Sustainability

Support Theory

Selecting timber is both an engineering and environmental choice. Plantation pine is popular because it’s light, easy to cut and regrows quickly, making it sustainable. Using recycled offcuts saves money and keeps wood out of landfill. Avoid rare hardwoods that take decades to replace. Good designers plan cuts to use every piece and think about how each material affects forests and carbon footprint.
