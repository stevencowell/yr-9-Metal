Week 11: CAD – Computer‑Aided Design

Support Theory

CAD software lets you draw your carry‑all on a computer screen. You can view it in 3‑D, spin it around and check sizes before you cut any wood. Digital models help spot problems early (like a handle hitting a lid) and can create cutting lists automatically. Popular student tools include Tinkercad, SketchUp or Fusion 360.
