Week 9: Clamping Techniques

Support Theory

Clamps hold glued joints tight until the adhesive cures. Use scrap blocks between the clamp jaws and timber to prevent dents. Place clamps on opposite sides and tighten them evenly. Too few clamps can leave gaps; over‑tightening can crush soft wood. Even pressure makes a strong, straight joint.
